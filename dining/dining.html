Lounge Bar

Step into our Lounge Bar for a relaxing ambiance. Enjoy a wide range of refreshing drinks and light snacks, perfectly designed for your casual meet-ups and chill-out moments. If you desire alcohol, we have a wide variety of drinks you can choose from that are made up by the bartender.

Choose across a huge range of comforting seats that are each unique. At night, the fireplace will be lighted with a real fire to provide you with a surreal and cozy experience near the fire, especially in the cold climate in the Swiss Alps.

Our expert mixologist curates a special cocktail menu each season, focusing on fresh, local ingredients and classic techniques. Whether you're a fan of traditional cocktails or you're looking for something a bit more adventurous, our menu is sure to delight.

Dining at the sky:Terrace Restaurant

Our 25-floor hotel boasts a terrace restaurant at the rooftop, where you can dine with the sun or the moon. Instead of serving heavy dishes, the terrace restaurant serves Swiss desserts and other light delicacies for you to slowly enjoy the food.

At night, you can enjoy the landscape extending from the top of the hotel, even the Swiss Alps, where the buildings down below illuminate the landscape with lights. Our Terrace Restaurant is the perfect place to unwind after a long day, with stunning views that make any dining experience unforgettable.

Customer Review: "Dining at the Terrace Restaurant was an amazing experience! The view is breathtaking and the desserts were absolutely delicious. A must-visit if you're staying at the hotel!"
