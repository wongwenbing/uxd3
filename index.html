San Marimo

Our Vision

YOUR NATURAL RESORT IN THE ALPINES.


Breathe in. Let your eyes savour the view of the cold, white Alps that line Switzerland. Feel the sheer power of the mountains overcome you. Whatever controls your daily life back home no longer troubles you here. This is where the Alps rule.


At San Marimo, we strive towards a mission for the splendor of life. And that ideal begins with a connection with nature. We use the best our region has to offer and further enhance it with a fine hint of luxury to satisfy our customers.




Our Three Missions.
The values we take pride in.



Connecting With Nature

At San Marimo, we believe that true luxury lies in the ability to reconnect with nature, to rediscover our place in the tapestry of life. So come, wander along our ethereal trails, breathe in the whispers of the forest, and let nature guide you on a transformative journey that will leave an indelible mark on your soul.

To Deliver Luxury

Welcome to San Marimo, where the pinnacle of opulence harmoniously merges with the pristine splendor of nature. As the epitome of luxury, we are devoted to delivering an unparalleled experience that transcends your wildest dreams.

Making New Memories

Whether it's a romantic getaway, a family celebration, or a personal retreat, let San Marimo provide an enchanting backdrop for your most cherished moments. We believe that every experience should be a celebration of life, an opportunity to savor the beauty of the present and forge lasting connections with loved ones.

About Our Region


It seems like Mother Nature was pulling out all the stops to flaunt all her magnificence on display at the Matterhorn. Its monumental peak towers high above the surrounding Alps. The immutable stone and blank snow sing of a tranquil aria.

Flora and fauna flourish in all their beauty. Embedded in these glorious fields of snow, woods and mountains is the San Marimo.

A destination for discovery and exploration. A sanctuary for peace and serenity.
